About Us

Surrounded by forest , mountains and water streams , Kipling Trail Cottage is 4km away from the city.The view is absolutely eye pleasing. Relax and rejuvenate amidst nature and gaze out into the greenery you are surrounded with, or the mountains that seem to be standing guard in the distance! We organise bonfires so you can spend a quiet evening toasting marshmallows and sipping on hot cup of coffee/tea , while the stream gushes past, with not a care in the world! a 4km Trek that starts from Rajpur dehradun leads up to the cottage, which is brightly lit wth lighting that causes the place to exude an inviting aura! Inside, the living room flaunts a rustic walls and wood-panelled flooring and wooden ceiling giving it luxurious feel. It is appointed with a indoor fireplace to keep you warm during cold nights. The bedrooms are beautifully decorated, with traditional rugs , a comfortable bed, and generous windows that let in the sunlight in the day and the mellow light of the stars in the night! Panels of stone and wood flank the bed, giving a unique design effect to the space. The attached bathrooms are hygienic , with modern sanitary ware, a large wall-mounted mirror.

Everyone has a dream and so did Vinay. While working as a Cabin Crew Supervisor for Jet Airways , Vinay always had a dream of retiring and settling somewhere away from the city life, surrounded by nature. Little did he know his dream would be converted to a reality sooner than anticipated. When Jet Airways abruptly stopped operations in 2019 , Vinay decided to take the opportunity to make his dream a reality. During this time, he and his wife Aastha went for a few hikes in Uttarakhand and Leh. It was during one of these hikes from Dehradun to Mussoorie that they came across the beautiful Talyanigaad village. Both of them were instantly taken by the picture-perfect valley, with its lush green landscape, water stream and waterfall. It was only 4 kms from Dehradun, but it was isolated, and most of the city's population had no idea that such a place existed nearby. Inspired by this beautiful spot, Vinay decided to build their dream homestay -Kipling Trail Cottage, with Aastha's support.

Things To Do

Trek

There are a few day treks near the cottage:

- Hike through the forest to Chamasari village (3 km one way)

- Walk by the stream and numerous waterfalls to reach Rajpur (3 km one way)

- Trek to Mussoorie via Barlowganj (8 km one way)

- Leisure walk to the Sunset Point (2 km one way)

- Walk through the forest to the ruins of houses of staff working in limestone mines (1.5 km from the cottage)
